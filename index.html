DUNDETT

ABOUT THE GAME

DUNDETT is a text adventure for the TI-84 Plus CE. Explore caves, befriend (or anger) a bear, avoid the grue, and discover the secrets hidden within.

  • Feed the bear, or face the consequences
  • Navigate pitch-black rooms without getting eaten
  • Find the office — it's weirder than it sounds
  • Each game has unique secrets
